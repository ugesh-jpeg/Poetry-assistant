In the yard, a playful dog, chased a frog into a bog.
Through the misty morning fog, he left his mark on a fallen log.

A cat with a fancy hat, sat on a soft, warm mat.
Along came a sneaky rat, with a bat that startled the cat.

By the old and sturdy tree, buzzed a happy, busy bee.
In the breeze, feeling free, the world was clear for all to see.

Up above, the endless sky, where dreams and wishes love to fly.
Waving the world a sweet bye, as the sun set, a hue so sly.
